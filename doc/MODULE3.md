### Traduza támbem as seguintes:

### **Viagens:**

1. **Informações Turísticas:**
   - *Informal:* "Can you recommend any must-see attractions in the area?"
   - *Formal:* "Could you kindly suggest notable attractions in the vicinity?"

2. **No Aeroporto:**
   - *Informal:* "Where's the baggage claim?"
   - *Formal:* "May I inquire about the location of the baggage claim area?"

3. **Reserva de Hotel:**
   - *Informal:* "I'd like to book a room for two nights, please."
   - *Formal:* "I would appreciate reserving accommodations for a duration of two nights."

### **História e Cultura:**

1. **Visita a Museus:**
   - *Informal:* "Let's check out that museum; it's supposed to be fascinating."
   - *Formal:* "Shall we visit the museum known for its captivating exhibits?"

2. **Explorando a Cidade:**
   - *Informal:* "We can wander around and soak in the local culture."
   - *Formal:* "We may stroll through the streets to absorb the indigenous culture."

3. **Perguntando Sobre a História:**
   - *Informal:* "Do you know the history behind this ancient site?"
   - *Formal:* "Could you enlighten me on the historical significance of this ancient site?"

### **Engenharia:**

1. **Projetando Estruturas:**
   - *Informal:* "Let's design a sturdy bridge with these specifications."
   - *Formal:* "We should draft plans for a resilient bridge incorporating these specifications."

2. **Solução de Problemas:**
   - *Informal:* "We need to figure out how to fix this issue with the circuit."
   - *Formal:* "It is imperative to devise a solution for rectifying the anomaly in the circuit."

### **Eletrônica:**

1. **Compra de Eletrônicos:**
   - *Informal:* "I'm thinking of getting a new smartphone."
   - *Formal:* "I am contemplating the acquisition of a new mobile communication device."

2. **Projetando Circuitos:**
   - *Informal:* "Let's build a circuit for the home automation system."
   - *Formal:* "We should engineer a circuit to facilitate the home automation system."
